About us

M&H.Co is a family of local clothing brands and businesses that allow Filipino customers to express themselves through fashion and design while also choosing a more sustainable lifestyle. By providing our customer offering and designing with a focus on sustainable and profitable growth, we create value for people and society in general.

To make the collection more sustainable for different seasons and beyond, the garments are made from recycled polyester and recycled polyamide.
Manila

The newest physical store of M&H.Co is in Manila, the center and the capital city of the Philippines, making it the ideal place to acquire exposure and promotion for our high-quality clothing.

Novaliches, QC

In early 2022, M&H.Co opened its first physical store designed to satisfy both digital and physical shopping in one location.

Project 8, QC

The Novaliches branch's success made it possible to open another physical store in Project 8 Quezon City. Many showed their love for Filipino made men and women dresses; hence the Project 8 branch has the most profitable sale in 2022.
