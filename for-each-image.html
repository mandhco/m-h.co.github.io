Measuring Dress Size

Not sure about your size? Follow these simple steps to figure it out:
Shoulder - Measure the shoulder at the back, from edge to edge with arms relaxed on both sides
Chest - Measure around the body under the arms at the fullest part of the chest with your arms relaxed at both sides.
Sleeve - Measure from the shoulder seam through the outer arm to the cuff/hem
Hip - Stand with your feet together and measure around the fullest part of your hips.
Waist - Measure around the narrowest part of your natural waistline, located above your belly button and below your rib cage.s
Length - Measured vertically from shoulder to the desired Length - Measure from the highest point of the shoulder seam to the bottom (for example Knee)
